Ranking

  • (1) Related to artificial intelligence methodology

    • First gear: NIPS, ICML, ICLR
    • It can be seen that the three top conferences are also the top three influencers in Google Scholar’s 2019 artificial intelligence publications!
    • Second gear: AAAI, IJCAI, COLT

  • (2) Application related

    • Computer Vision CVPR, ECCV, ICCV (Top 3 in Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition

  • (3) Natural language processing

    • ACL, EMNLP, NAACL (also the 3 giants

The First Class

  • IJCAI

    • AI’s best comprehensive meeting
    • International Joint Conferences on Artificial Intelligence
    • Starting in 1969, it is opened every two years, and it is opened in odd years. Because AI is too big,
    • So although each session can basically record more than 100 (now more than 200),
    • But there are few articles in each field,
    • A field as large as machine learning and computer vision is about 10 articles each time.

  • AAAI

    • American Association for Artificial Intelligence AAAI Annual Meeting
    • Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence
    • It is a good meeting, but its grade is not stable, it can be given as 1, can also be given as 1- or 2,
    • In general I give it “1”. This is because its opening method is completely restricted by IJCAI:
    • Open every year, but if the IJCAI of this year is held in North America, then it will be closed. So,
    • In even years, because there is no IJCAI, it is the best AI comprehensive meeting,

  • COLT

    • This is the best meeting of computational learning theory
    • Association for Computational Learning
    • Hosted by ACM, held annually. Computational learning theory can basically be seen as the intersection of theoretical computer science and machine learning,
    • So this will be regarded by some people as a theoretical computer science meeting rather than an AI meeting.
    • A friend of mine portrayed it wonderfully in one sentence: “A small group of mathematicians are meeting”.

  • CVPR

    • One of the best conferences in computer vision and pattern recognition
    • IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition
    • Sponsored by IEEE, held annually. Although there is computer vision on the topic,
    • But personally think that its pattern recognition tastes heavier.
    • In fact it should be the best meeting for pattern recognition,

  • ICCV

    • One of the best in computer vision
    • International Conference on Computer Vision
    • Hosted by IEEE, held annually.

  • ICML

    • One of the best conferences in machine learning
    • International Conference on Machine Learning is now hosted by IMLS, held annually. See the introduction about NIPS

  • NIPS

    • One of the best conferences in neural computing
    • Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems
    • Hosted by NIPS, held annually.
    • The name of the conference is “Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems”,
    • So, unlike “standard” machine learning conferences like ICML \ ECML,
    • There is a considerable part of the content of neuroscience in NIPS, and there is a certain distance from machine learning.
    • But because the main content of the meeting is machine learning,
    • Or it is closely related to machine learning, so many people regard NIPS as one of the best conferences in machine learning.

  • ACL

    • The best conference on computational linguistics / natural language processing
    • Association for Computational Linguistics
    • Hosted by ACL (Association of Computational Linguistics), open every year …

  • KR

    • One of the best conferences in knowledge representation and reasoning
    • Principles of Knowledge Representation
    • In fact, it is also one of the best conferences of traditional AI (that is, logic-based AI)

  • SIGIR

    • The best meeting in information retrieval
    • Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval
    • Hosted by ACM, it opens every year. This meeting is getting more and more popular in small circles now.
    • Information retrieval should not be considered AI, but because more and more machine learning is used in it,
    • In recent years, it has even tasted like a machine learning application conference, so it is also included.

  • SIGKDD

    • The best meeting in information retrieval
    • The International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining
    • The best conference on data exploration, sponsored by ACM, is held every year. The history of this conference is relatively short,
    • After all, compared with other fields, data exploration is still only a little brother or even a nephew.
    • It was difficult to list it in tier-1 a few years ago. On the one hand, it was far less famous than other top conferences.
    • On the other hand, it is relatively easy to be hired.
    • But now it should be listed in tier-1 should be no doubt. Another: see the introduction of sir and lucky

  • UAI

    • The name is “Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence”, which involves many aspects such as representation \ reasoning \ learning
